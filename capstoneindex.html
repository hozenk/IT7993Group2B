Design Science Research in Information Systems and Technology

Menu: Home | Database | Resources

The purpose of this site is to act as a referential resource for the Design Science Research in Information Systems and Technology organization website. The database tool allows the user to search through a collection of all the design science literature resources used by the organization. In order to use the tool, navigate to the 'Database' tab from the menu at the top of the page to get started.
